Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance, especially for popular time slots. However, we often have same-day availability, so feel free to call us.

What should I wear to my appointment?

For massage treatments, you can undress to your comfort level. We provide robes and ensure complete privacy. For other services, comfortable clothing is recommended.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! We offer gift certificates for all our services and packages. They can be purchased online or in-person and are valid for one year from the purchase date.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a 50% charge of the service fee.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans for massage therapy services. Please bring your insurance card and we'll help you verify coverage.

Can I book for a group?

Absolutely! We offer group packages and can accommodate corporate wellness events, bridal parties, and other group bookings. Contact us for special rates.
