Our Story

Founded in 2019, RelaxAtSume began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sanctuary where individuals could escape the stresses of modern life and reconnect with their inner peace.

Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, recognized the growing need for authentic wellness experiences in Toronto's fast-paced environment. After years of working in corporate wellness and witnessing the toll that stress takes on people's health and happiness, she decided to create something different.

What started as a small practice with just two treatment rooms has grown into a comprehensive wellness center that serves hundreds of clients each month. Our commitment to excellence, personalized care, and continuous learning has made us a trusted name in the Canadian wellness community.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional wellness experiences that restore balance, reduce stress, and enhance the overall quality of life for our clients.

Our Vision

To be Canada's leading wellness destination, recognized for our innovative treatments, expert care, and transformative results.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, excellence, and a genuine commitment to each client's unique wellness journey.

Personalized Care

Every treatment is customized to your specific needs, preferences, and wellness goals. We take the time to understand your unique situation and create a plan that works for you.

Evidence-Based Practices

Our treatments are grounded in scientific research and proven therapeutic techniques. We stay current with the latest developments in wellness and massage therapy.
